    • mm_mamba Multi-modal fusion in PyTorch for diverse data.

      • block.py MultiModal Mamba Fusion in PyTorch
      • model.py Multi-modal modeling with fusion techniques.
    • model_example.py Imports torch, creates MMM model with parameters, passes x and img.
